Key Features

Real-time Analytics

Get instant feedback on your typing speed (WPM), accuracy percentage, and error count as you type.

Multiple Difficulty Levels

Choose from Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert levels with different text complexities and challenges.

Progress Tracking

Track your improvement over time with detailed statistics and visual progress charts.

Various Text Categories

Practice with common words, famous quotes, programming terms, literature, or random sentences.

Customizable Timers

Test your speed with different time limits from 30 seconds to 3 minutes based on your preference.

Performance Scoring

Receive a comprehensive score that combines both speed and accuracy for balanced improvement.
