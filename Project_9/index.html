Key Features

Spaced Repetition

Our algorithm schedules reviews at optimal intervals to maximize retention and minimize study time.

Organize your flashcards into different decks for various subjects, topics, or courses.

Progress Tracking

Monitor your learning progress with detailed statistics and visual progress indicators.

Responsive Design

Study on any device - the app works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile screens.

Local Storage

Your data is saved locally in your browser - no account required, complete privacy.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Navigate quickly with keyboard shortcuts for flipping cards and marking difficulty.
