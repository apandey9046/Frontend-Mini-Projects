Project Overview

Experience the timeless classic Tic Tac Toe with a modern twist! Play against a smart AI opponent with multiple difficulty levels, or challenge a friend in two-player mode. With beautiful animations, sound effects, and comprehensive statistics tracking, this game brings new life to the traditional pencil-and-paper game.

Statistics Tracking

Track your wins, losses, and draws with persistent statistics that save between sessions.

Beautiful Animations

Enjoy smooth animations for moves, win celebrations, and dynamic winning line drawings.

Sound Effects

Immersive sound effects for moves, wins, and draws enhance the gaming experience.

Mobile Optimized

Fully responsive design with touch-friendly controls that work perfectly on all devices.
